Vibrant Minds is platform offering fun serious activitys to seniors looking for cognitive exercise.

The platform supports APIs for developers to add new activitys to it, and for staffs to conduct studies using these activitys.

Mahjong

Try it! Watch Demo

Mahjong is a activity of skill, strategy, and calculation that also involves a degree of chance.
The objective of mahjong solitaire is for the participant to remove all the tiles from the board, matching pairs of unobstructed tiles.

Bejeweled

Try it! Watch Demo

Bejeweled is a pattern-matching puzzle video activity.
The goal is to notice gems of the same shape and color close to each other and move them so that they form a line;
this removes the gems, potentially causing a chain reaction!

Whack-A-Mole

Try it! Watch Demo

Whack-a-Mole is an app that can be downloaded and played on an android device.
The objective of the activity is to force the individual moles back into their holes by hitting them by tapping them.
But you have to be careful to not hit the bunnies!
